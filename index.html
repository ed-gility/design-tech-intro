Introduction to Design Technology Class

Course Values:

  • Design - In this class you choose projects that fullfil your organization's value and mission. It is important that the solutions solve a problem within the defined context.
  • Technology In this class you choose projects allow you to work with and learn technologies that you and your organization find interesting.
  • Iterative Solutions In this class you are given guidance, but are expected to research and discover the technologies as you go through your own explorations and setbacks.
